Friday Night Dinner

Two siblings belonging to an eccentric family, join their parents to celebrate the sabbath with a peaceful dinner every Friday night. However, despite great care, things always turn chaotic. It seems a simple premise but the show makes this event turn so many different ways with witty jabs and comic turn of events that you keep wondering how they are able to pull off the same setting into new jokes season after season.
 
If youre up for a bit of situational humour and some adoscent laughs then Friday Night Dinner is recommended to watch with mates or alone if you fancy some silly laughs.
 
"**** - 4 Stars"
-Ayush
