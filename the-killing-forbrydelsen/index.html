”The Killing” / Forbrydelsen - *****

"One of my Favourite TV Murder Mysteries."

-  Ayush

Set in the cold, civilized, but often dark lands of Scandinavian this gripping thriller is miles ahead of it's American remake.

This is the Danish series that started the ScandiNoir trend for me.

There are scenes in Forbrydelsen that will haunt you, and the slow paced thriller slowly builds up to its finish with plenty of episodes to binge watch for a week.
