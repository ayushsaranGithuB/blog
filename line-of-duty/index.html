Line of Duty

When Steve Arnott is transferred to a police anti-corruption unit, he finds his target is the city's top detective, Tony Gates. Can Gates really be as good as he appears? Arnott must engage in a cat-and-mouse struggle to uncover Gates' secret.

This fast-paced undercover series follows AC-12, an elite anti corruption unit and its investigations into police cover-ups and conspiracies. Every Season follows a new lead and a new setup making for enjoyable binging, season after season.

Everything about this show is top-notch. Writing, pacing, character-development.
5 Stars - Ayush
