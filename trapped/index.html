Trapped

Another subtitled murder mystery, this time from Iceland.

A dismembered body is found in the river at the same time a ferry arrives at a small fishing port prompting a lockdown and investigation, But to make things worse as a bad snowstorm is threatening to cut off the town from all outside help and the under-staffed local police chief is under pressure to find answers before the severe weather forces him to set everyone, including the murderer loose.

Great pacing and the cold, dark, gritty setting makes for extra magic with the snow-filled fjords for backdrop.
