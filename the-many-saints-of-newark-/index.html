The Many Saints of Newark - ***

Young Anthony Soprano is growing up in one of the most tumultuous eras in Newark's history, becoming a man just as rival gangsters begin to rise up and challenge the all-powerful DiMeo crime family's hold over the increasingly race-torn city.
 
This feature-length prequel to the Sopranos explores the story of the childhood and adolescence of the series’ troubled anti-hero, Tony Soprano. Played by his real-life son the story is set in the late 60s and explores the landscape of New Jersey that shaped the mob into what it became.
 
On it's own it doesn't hold up as a gangster movie, but long time Sorpranos fans will get a kick out of the casting and backstories which keep this movie slowly trudging along
 
*** - Ayush
