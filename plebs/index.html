Plebs

Plebs is about 3 regular guys who move to the big city and follows their adventures as they juggle love, friendship and work, except the show is set in the first century and the city they move to is Ancient Rome.

If you're a fan of 'The In-Betweeners' and 'Friday Night Dinner' then this one is 5 Stars also, atleast for the first few seasons.
